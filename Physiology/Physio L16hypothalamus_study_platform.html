Connections Overview
Afferent (Inputs)

What feeds INTO the hypothalamus

Cerebral cortex, Limbic system, Thalamus, Basal ganglia, Brain stem reticular formation, Ascending sensory pathways

High Yield
Efferent (Outputs)

What the hypothalamus controls

Thalamus → Cortex, Limbic system, Brain stem reticular, Autonomic centers, Respiratory/vasomotor centers, Posterior pituitary

High Yield
ANS Regulation ⚠️

Autonomic Control

Posterior → Sympathetic: ↑HR, ↑ABP, pupil dilate, ↑catecholamines
Anterior → Parasympathetic: ↓HR, ↓ABP, pupil constrict, ↑peristalsis/salivation

⚠️ EXAM MCQ
Temperature

Thermoregulation

Preoptic area: Thermostat neurons
Posterior nuclei: Heat GAIN (shivering, vasoconstriction, ↑T3/T4)
Anterior nuclei: Heat LOSS (sweating, vasodilation)

⚠️ EXAM
Endocrine Control

Pituitary Regulation

Anterior pituitary: via portal blood (releasing/inhibiting factors)
Posterior pituitary: via nerve tracts — ADH (S.O nucleus) + Oxytocin (P.V nucleus)

⚠️ EXAM MCQ
Circadian Rhythm

Biological Master Clock

SCN receives light/dark info DIRECTLY from the retina. Controls sleep-wake, body temp, hormone secretion over 24h diurnal cycle.

⚠️ EXAM Written Q
Limbic System

Emotion & Behavior

Cortical: cingulate gyrus, parahippocampal gyrus
Subcortical: amygdala, hippocampus, hypothalamus
Papez circuit = emotional memory loop

⚠️ EXAM Written Q
Hypoglycemia Response

Counter-regulatory Mechanism

Glucoreceptors detect low glucose → ↑ Adrenaline + GH + Cortisol → Glycogenolysis + Gluconeogenesis to restore blood glucose
