Diagnostic Algorithm — Glaucoma
Patient: Eye pain + halos + blurred vision + headache
↓
Tonometry → Measure IOP (Normal: 15-20 mmHg)
↓
IOP > 20 mmHg confirmed → Glaucoma suspected
↓
Gonioscopy: inspect iridocorneal angle
↓
Open angle? → Excess production → Acetazolamide
↓
Closed angle? → Blocked drainage → Parasympathomimetics / Surgery
↓
Monitor: prevent optic disc atrophy & blindness
