Extraocular Nerve Lesion Pattern
CN VI — Nuclear vs Peripheral Lesion
🔴 Nuclear Lesion (Facial Colliculus)
  • Ipsilateral lateral rectus palsy
  • Ipsilateral facial (CN VII) palsy
  • Both caused simultaneously
  • Location: Pons (facial colliculus)
🟢 Peripheral Lesion
  • Ipsilateral lateral rectus palsy only
  • No facial palsy
  • Internal strabismus (medial squint)
  • Unopposed medial rectus action
