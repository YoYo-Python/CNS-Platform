Anatomy of the Orbit & Ocular Innervation

Complete guide covering orbital bones, extraocular muscles, blood supply, venous drainage, and all nerve supplies. High-yield exam material.

⚠️ Walls always in exam ⚠️ LR6 SO4 Rule 🩺 Cavernous Sinus Danger 🧠 Nasociliary Branches ⚡ Ophthalmic Artery = End Artery

🏗️ Orbital Walls

Four walls — every single wall is exam material.

🔼 Roof

  • Orbital plate of Frontal bone
  • Lesser wing of Sphenoid

🔽 Floor

  • Orbital plate of Maxilla (main)
  • Zygomatic bone
  • Orbital process of Palatine

◀️ Medial Wall

  • Frontal process of Maxilla
  • Lacrimal bone
  • Orbital plate of Ethmoid
  • Body of Sphenoid

▶️ Lateral Wall

  • Zygomatic bone
  • Greater wing of Sphenoid
